About Me

I’m Muhammad Sabeel — a 3D artist and interior designer specializing in realistic, modern, and detail-focused visualizations. I work across product rendering, interior design, and architectural visualization, helping brands and businesses present their ideas with clarity and strong visual impact.

From clean product renders for websites to full interior concepts and architectural scenes, I focus on delivering high-quality, accurate, and visually appealing results. My work is backed by experience with brands worldwide, ensuring each project is handled with professionalism, creativity, and attention to detail.
